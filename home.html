Duas for the Journey Ahead

رَبِّ هَبْ لِي مِن لَّدُنكَ ذُرِّيَّةً طَيِّبَةً ۖ إِنَّكَ سَمِيعُ الدُّعَاءِ

"My Lord, grant me from Yourself a good offspring. Indeed, You are the Hearer of supplication" - [Quran 3:38]

Shared by Fatima

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْتَوْدِعُكَ دِينِي وَأَمَانَتِي وَخَوَاتِيمَ عَمَلِي

"O Allah, I entrust to You my faith, my trust, and the conclusions of my deeds."

- Aisha

May Allah bless your child with good health, happiness, and a kind heart. May they grow to be the coolness of your eyes and a source of continuous blessings. May your journey through motherhood be filled with joy and precious moments.

- Your Team
